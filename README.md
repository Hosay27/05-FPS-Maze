# 05-FPS-Maze
3D first person shooter

8 April 2023

Controls:
 ESC or Tab to pause
 
 WASD to move
 
 LMB or Space to shoot
 
 E to pick up or drop your weapon
 
 B to open inventory

## Implementation
- most models made by me

- 2 levels

- music and sound effects
